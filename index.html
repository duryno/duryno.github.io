Our Goals for the Course

Juraj


  1. What I'd like to be able to build at the end of this course is a dashboard similar to the one in Google Analytics which will display stats relevant to content of the travel startup I'm building. Specifically, it should be possible to display the number of views of a particular attraction during a specified time period (with days being the smallest unit), number of likes/dislikes of the attraction, its average rating, average time spent by the user at the attraction's page, number of photos and stories related to the attraction, etc. Similarly, it should be possible to display similar data for stories that the users can write.
  2. The visualisation should describe the engagement of users of all apps (web and Android) in one place. Moreover, it shall show patterns in user behaviour and popularity of places/stories in relation to others.
  3. The dashboard described above should be used primarily by myself, others involved in the company and the company partners. This means most users are expected to have a pretty good understanding of the platform and of the visualised data.
